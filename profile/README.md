# African Travellers Main Page
African Travellers is a next-generation Corporate Travel OS, purpose-built for Africa’s unique business realities. Unlike global solutions like SAP Concur or TravelPerk—designed for North American and European markets—we solve the pain points that African businesses face daily.
We enable seamless expense management, booking, policy compliance, and analytics with features tailored for the continent:

- **Expense capture** via mobile camera (OCR + AI categorization) and reimbursements through **M-Pesa, MTN MoMo, Airtel Money, or bank transfers.**
- **Multi-currency support** with real-time FX rates for 50+ African currencies.
- **Offline-first functionality** for low-connectivity environments, with automatic sync when back online.
- **Local transport integration** (matatus, boda bodas, intercity buses) and support for handwritten receipts.
- **Corporate policy enforcement** at booking, with approver workflows and duty-of-care tracking.

Our platform empowers **pan-African corporations, NGOs, and SMEs** to streamline travel, reduce costs, and maintain compliance—without the friction of tools built for Western markets.

**Why African Travellers?**

Because Africa deserves a travel solution that works for Africa.
